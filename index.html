Welcome to the Fabulous Wild Circus!

Meet the most extraordinary circus artists!

We are on the road since 2003, we want to show to you a fabulous artists, acrobats, tamers, jugglers that the world has better.

Juggling !!
Come and discover Alicia & Alfred! The fabulous jugglers!

This is two young jugglers from Italia, They bring their expertise to all of junglage through various accessory from tennis racket until finely sharpened knives

Taming !!
Come shiver with Shira! The wonderful lion tamer!

Shira comes from Romania, her father was a famous tamer in his country and naturally she resumed the continuation of his shows.

Flying trapeze !!
Vertiginous Fabricio! The fabulous flying man!

Fabricio comes from Poland, his first appearances in public will immediately forge his taste for the spectacle (and the risk), through his function of stuntman in the Doros show.
